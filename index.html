Scoring notes

The app uses transparent heuristic buckets instead of a single black-box score. No one bucket is universally best in every context.

Survival

Health, stamina sustain, regen, resistances, and survivability.

Melee

Melee damage, melee speed, return melee damage, and critical damage.

Ranged

Projectile damage, reload speed, charge speed, explosive damage, and critical damage.

Exploration

Mobility, oxygen/water economy, encumbrance relief, cave sickness, and environmental support.

XP / Support

XP gained, shared XP, and tamed creature progression value.

Efficiency

How much value a recipe gives relative to complexity and cost.

Recipe Rankings

Scout strong recipes before building a combo. Rankings come from the current scoring model and your Carnivore setting.
